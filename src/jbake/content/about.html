Mike McGarr is a Senior Software Engineer on the Netflix Engineering Tools team. He has been developing Java/JVM-based applications for most of my career, but has been known to dabble in other languages as well. He is passionate about building quality software through automation. You can frequently find him talking about Agile, Continuous Delivery, DevOps, or build and test automation. Mike is the founder of the DC Continuous Delivery meetup.

Mike began my career in the Washington, D.C. metro area as a consultant for the federal government. He started out at Booz Allen Hamilton where he cut my teeth building software solutions for the Marine Corps and the National Science Foundation. His passion to build automation, continuous integration and test automation was cultivated here. In 2009 he joined Excella Consulting, where he was immersed in the world of Agile software development. At Excella, he began to his Continuous Delivery journey, automating deployment pipelines for customers.

In 2012, he moved out of consulting and joined Blackboard, Inc. as the Director of Engineering Operations. As Director of EngOps, Mike was responsible for the build and test infrastructure, as well as the development tooling for Blackboard's flagship product, Learn. He also worked to migrate Blackboard Engineering towards Continuous Delivery, by coaching senior management, engineering and QA.

In 2014, he moved my family to the Bay Area to join Netflix's Engineering Tools team, as a Senior Software Engineer. At Netflix, Mike is responsible for helping engineering teams adopt the tools available, and make the migration process as simple as possible.
