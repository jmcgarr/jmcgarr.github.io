One of the challenges we have on the Blackboard Learn product development team is scaling up our Jenkins instance.  Are we modularize our product, the need to many small build pipelines has increased.  Managing these projects through a small Ops team is not a scalable solution.  I wanted to provide a self-service function that engineers could utilize to add a new project to Jenkins.  Enter the Jenkins Job DSL plugin.
